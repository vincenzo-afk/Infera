# 01 — Vision and Goals

## Long-Term Vision
ChaosVoice aims to become the go-to lightweight, root-free real-time voice-effects app for entertainment, streaming, and social calls — recognized for its distinctive "chaotic/demonic" sound character rather than generic pitch-shift effects.

## Design Philosophy
- **Transparency first:** the user is always shown clearly when their voice is being altered. No hidden or silent activation.
- **Local-only processing:** all DSP runs on-device. No audio is ever sent to a server.
- **Root-free by design:** the app should never require root, system-signed builds, or Accessibility Service abuse to function. It works within Android's standard security model.
- **Expressive over subtle:** effects are meant to be bold and characterful, not naturalistic voice cleanup.
- **Low friction:** one tap to activate, sliders for anyone who wants deeper control.

## Non-Goals
- Not a covert surveillance or impersonation tool
- Not designed to alter another person's voice without their own device running the app
- Not a machine-learning voice-cloning product (no training on target voices)
- Not intended to require or encourage rooting a device

## Future Ideas
- Optional ML-based voice conversion as an advanced, clearly-labeled alternative mode
- Cross-platform desktop companion for streamers
- Community preset sharing
- Root-only "advanced mode" for power users, clearly separated from the default experience
